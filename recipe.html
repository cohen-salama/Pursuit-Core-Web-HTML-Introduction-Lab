Tarta Tricolor

Description

This particular dish is famous in Argentina because it's delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spinach
  • 1 pound cheese
  • 1 pound pumpkin
  • 1 pre-made tart crust

Directions

  1. Bake the pumpkin for half an hour
  2. Cut the cheese in cubes
  3. Put the tart crust in an oven tray
  4. Throw ingredients in layers inside
  5. Bake for another half hour
